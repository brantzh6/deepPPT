# Quality Bar

## Content Quality Checks
- Is the page making a clear claim?
- Is the claim supported by facts or justified interpretation?
- Does the page match the audience level?
- Does it overstate current capability?
- Does it state enterprise boundaries and risks where needed?

## Story Quality Checks
- Does each chapter answer a concrete question?
- Is there a clean progression from context to implication to action?
- Is any page redundant?
- Are key transitions explicit?
- Is the ending sharper than the beginning?

## Visual Quality Checks
- Is there a visual anchor?
- Is the page weight centered intentionally?
- Is there too much empty space?
- Is the layout generic or page-specific?
- Are the typography levels clear?
- Does the page feel like a decision document or a template dump?

## Export Quality Checks
- No overflow
- No cut-off elements
- No encoding issues
- No footer or page-number collisions
- No screenshot artifacts
- PPTX opens correctly
- Page count matches expected sequence

## Final Acceptance Standard
A slide is acceptable only if:
- it is narratively necessary
- it is visually intentional
- it is factually defensible
- it is locally revisable
