# Final Acceptance Checklist

## Content
- Each slide has one clear claim.
- Claims are supported by facts or justified interpretation.
- Enterprise boundaries and risks are stated where needed.

## Story
- Each chapter answers a concrete question.
- Page order progresses from context to implication to action.
- No slide is redundant.

## Visual
- Important slides have a visual anchor.
- Weight center is intentional.
- Large empty containers are treated as defects.
- Layout feels page-specific, not template-generic.

## Export
- No overflow.
- No cut-off objects.
- No encoding issues.
- PPTX opens cleanly.
- Page sequence matches expected output.

## Final Rule
A slide is accepted only if it is narratively necessary, visually intentional, factually defensible, and locally revisable.
