# Final Acceptance Checklist

## Content
- Each slide has one clear claim.
- Claims are supported by facts or justified interpretation.
- Enterprise boundaries and risks are stated where needed.

## Story
- Each chapter answers a concrete question.
- Page order progresses from context to implication to action.
- No slide is redundant.

## Visual
- Important slides have a visual anchor.
- Weight center is intentional.
- Large empty containers are treated as defects.
- Layout feels page-specific, not template-generic.

## Layout Standards (Cross-Page-Type)
- **Shared Boundaries**: Left and right content areas share the same vertical boundaries (contentTop/contentBottom).
- **Title Alignment**: Section titles on left and right sides align to the same baseline.
- **Spacing Consistency**: Uniform spacing between related elements within a page type.
- **No Overlap**: Text elements must not overlap with shapes or other text.
- **Visual Hierarchy**: Primary content (visual anchor) dominates; secondary content supports without competing.

## Export
- No overflow.
- No cut-off objects.
- No encoding issues.
- PPTX opens cleanly.
- Page sequence matches expected output.
- No severe element overlaps (overlap area > 0.1 inches²).

## Final Rule
A slide is accepted only if it is narratively necessary, visually intentional, factually defensible, and locally revisable.
